When I first started studying Computer Engineering, I was excited but I was also full of doubt. I kept wondering if I was smart enough, if I truly belonged in this field. That feeling was uncomfortable, but it was also honest.What I've learned so far is that doubt is part of the process. Computer Engineering is challenging by design it pushes you to think differently, solve problems creatively, and keep going even when things don't make sense right away. Every small breakthrough has made the struggle worth it.I'm still at the beginning of this journey, and I have a lot to learn. But I'm showing up, staying curious, and trusting the process. If you're just starting out too whatever your path know that it's okay not to have it all figured out. Just keep going.
